Overview

Welcome to Learning Creative Learning! We are very excited to have you!

This week includes an intro to the course, discussion of the Lifelong Kindergarten approach and sharing a childhood object based on Seymour Papert’s Gears of My Childhood essay.

To get started, head over to the first section - watch and read!

Throughout the week, you’ll have a chance to look through the materials at your own pace, but will also hold synchronous, live video calls on Unhangouts at, which you can check out om the live events page
